CVX-20 Inspired Design

The CVX-20 is an all-time favorite for 70's jet drive ski boats. The low profile and design elements from Italian super cars of the era and a powerplant from Detroit mated to a Berkeley jet drive make the Glastron-Carlson CVX-20 a true muscle boat.
